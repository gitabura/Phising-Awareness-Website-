Types of Phishing Attacks

Phishing attacks can happen through emails, text messages, phone calls, and even social media. Knowing the different types helps you stay safe.

Email Phishing

Fake emails pretending to be from trusted companies asking you to click links or share personal information.

Smishing

Phishing through SMS messages containing fake links or urgent requests.

Vishing

Phone calls from scammers pretending to be bank officials, police, or customer support.

Spear Phishing

A targeted attack aimed at a specific person or organization using personalized information.

Whaling

Phishing attacks targeting senior executives or business owners to steal sensitive information.
