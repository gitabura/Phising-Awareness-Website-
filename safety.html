Safety Tips to Prevent Phishing

Follow these simple cybersecurity practices to protect yourself from phishing attacks and keep your personal information secure.

🔑 Use Strong Passwords

Create long, unique passwords using a combination of letters, numbers, and symbols.

📲 Enable Two-Factor Authentication

Add an extra layer of security by enabling 2FA on your important accounts.

📧 Verify Emails

Always check the sender's email address before opening attachments or clicking links.
