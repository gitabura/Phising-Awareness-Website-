What is Phishing?

Phishing is a cyber attack in which attackers pretend to be trusted people or organizations to trick users into revealing sensitive information such as passwords, bank details, or personal data.

🎣 Fake Emails

Attackers send emails that appear to come from banks, companies, or government organizations.

🔗 Fake Websites

Victims are redirected to websites that look genuine but are designed to steal login credentials.

📱 Fake Messages

SMS messages may contain malicious links asking users to verify accounts or claim rewards.

⚠️ Goal of Phishing

The goal is to steal confidential information, money, or gain unauthorized access to accounts.
